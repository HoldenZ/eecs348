EECS 348 Lab Seven Practice Two

Once upon a time, there was a potato who dreamed of becoming a french fry. But every time he tried to get crispy and golden,
he ended up getting burnt and mushy. So, he decided to attend a fry-cooking class to learn the tricks of the trade.
Unfortunately, he got paired with a carrot who kept whispering "you're too soft" every time the potato made a mistake.
Despite the carrot's negativity, the potato persevered and finally became a delicious french fry. And as for the carrot,
well, he ended up getting turned into a salad. The end.
